Arrest Procedure Guide

Standardized arrest flow for Grand RP officers

  1. Establish Probable Cause
    Confirm crime via witness, evidence, or observation.
  2. Detain the Suspect
    Use cuffs and ensure scene safety.
  3. Search the Individual
    Confiscate illegal or relevant items.
  4. Read Miranda Rights
    Required before questioning.
  5. Penal Code Charges
    Use Penal Lookup to determine charges.
  6. Transport to PD
    Process arrest and submit report.

Important Notes

  • Always activate bodycam
  • Stack charges appropriately
  • No powergaming or metagaming
